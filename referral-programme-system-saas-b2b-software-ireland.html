INTRODUCTION TO THE REFERRAL PROGRAMME PORTAL




The referral programme portal system consists of the following main functionalities:

Leads & Referrers

Send invites, manage referrers accounts, update leads status, send & receive instant email notifications for new referrals and updates, from any device.


Personalised

Personalised access with your brand and social network links (Facebook, LinkedIn & Twitter ). Create Marketing Campaigns in your social networks and link them to your referral programme.


Manage Rewards

Create and Manage Rewards for your referrers. Publish rewards in any sector and location to find potential referrers. Create Marketing Campaigns in your social networks and link them to your referral programme.


Referrers Portal

Private access to the portal to manage their referrals, view updatesetc
