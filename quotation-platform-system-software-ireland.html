INTRODUCTION TO THE QUOTATION SYSTEM



Basic structure of our quotation management system:

Management of user accounts

There are three types of users accounts: administrator, manager and employee. Each of these roles has access to different functionalities.


Products management

Products can be added in two ways: automatically, with a template in excel format, or manually.




Management of Clients and Contacts.

Clients and Contacts can be added in two ways: automatically, with a template in excel format, or manually.





Quota management

A wizard guides the user through the necessary elements to create a quota:
  • 1. Customer and contact + annotations.
  • 2. Products + user assignment + annotations.
  • 3. Review of the quota. Available to the client in PDF format.

The list of quotas can be filtered, re-used, edited,, etc.





Quota history

All events carried out in the same quota are registered to later be consulted in the history; here you can see any change in the quota status.

Graphs and charts

The administrator and manager user accounts have access to a special tool to view the evolution of quotas, download in excel format, and filter by various criteria: date, user, client, status, etc.
